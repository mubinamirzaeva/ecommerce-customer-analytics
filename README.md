# E-Commerce Customer Analytics

A business analyst portfolio project analyzing ~100K orders from Olist, a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace, spanning 2016-2018.

**Status:** 🚧 In progress — actively building

## Business questions
1. Who are our most valuable customers, and which segments drive revenue?
2. How is the business growing, and where?
3. What drives customer satisfaction (and dissatisfaction)?
4. Where should the business invest to grow?

## Tech stack
- **PostgreSQL** for data storage and analytical queries
- **Python (pandas, matplotlib)** for exploratory analysis
- **Tableau Public** for executive dashboards

## Dataset
[Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce) — 9 related tables covering orders, customers, sellers, products, payments, and reviews.

Data is not included in this repo (~45MB, available on Kaggle). See `data/README.md` for setup instructions.
